Frequently Asked Questions

A landing page is a specific web page created to focus on providing information, generating leads, or making sales. The main focus of the landing page is to have a clear call to action.

A well-designed landing page with clear call to action (CTAs) attracts users to contact your company, fill out forms, or sign up for newsletters. This helps with effective lead generation.

Important metrics like conversion rate, bounce rate, average session length, and the quantity of form submissions or sales can help in determining the effectiveness and success of your landing page.
